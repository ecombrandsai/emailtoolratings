Independent star ratings for every email marketing tool. Part of the EmailToolAdviser network.

How we rate

Every platform is scored on seven weighted criteria: ease of use (20%), deliverability (20%), price (15%), support (15%), templates (10%), automation (10%), integrations (10%). Weighted scores roll up to a five-star aggregate.

How we make money

Affiliate commissions. Rankings are independent — several tools we rate poorly pay larger commissions than our top picks.
