About James

James Calloway leads EmailToolAdviser's deliverability, automation, and technical comparison coverage. He focuses on the variables that actually move small-business revenue: list quality, inbox placement, automation depth, and integration footprint. James's comparisons cite public industry benchmarks (Litmus deliverability reports, DMA email ROI studies, the Email Sender & Provider Coalition's inbox placement research) and label editorial opinion as editorial opinion — never as measured fact. He does not accept compensation tied to specific platform recommendations and partner influence has no impact on his analysis.

Editorial standards

James Calloway follows EmailToolAdviser's editorial standards: public benchmark data is cited with sources; editorial opinion is labeled as editorial opinion; first-party measurement claims are only made when first-party measurement was actually conducted (which is rarely the case for individual software platforms). Reader trust depends on this distinction.
